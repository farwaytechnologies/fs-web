Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right plan for my needs?

Consider the number of devices you need to protect and the level of security features you require. For basic protection on a single device, Antivirus Plus is sufficient. For multiple devices with advanced protection, Internet Security or Total Security would be better options.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The price difference between your current plan and the new plan will be prorated based on the remaining subscription period.

Do you offer monthly payment options?

Yes, we offer monthly payment options for all our plans. However, annual subscriptions provide better value with significant discounts compared to monthly payments.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise plans, we also offer invoice payment options.
