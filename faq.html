Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Farway Security products, services, and features.

Product Features

What types of threats does Farway Security protect against?
Farway Security provides comprehensive protection against various threats including malware, ransomware, phishing attacks, zero-day exploits, and advanced persistent threats (APTs). Our real-time scanning and behavioral analysis ensure your systems stay protected against emerging threats.
Does Farway Security slow down my computer?
No, Farway Security is designed to be lightweight and efficient. Our advanced optimization techniques ensure minimal impact on system performance while maintaining maximum protection. The software uses intelligent scanning that adapts to your system's resources.
Can I use Farway Security on multiple devices?
Yes, Farway Security offers multi-device protection. Depending on your subscription plan, you can protect multiple devices including PCs, Macs, smartphones, and tablets. Check our pricing page for specific device limits per plan.

Licensing & Subscription

How do I renew my subscription?
You can renew your subscription through your account dashboard or by contacting our sales team. We offer automatic renewal options for convenience, and you'll receive notifications before your subscription expires.
Can I transfer my license to another device?
Yes, you can transfer your license to a new device by deactivating it on the old device first. Simply log into your account dashboard and follow the device management instructions.

Technical Support

How do I contact technical support?
Our technical support team is available 24/7 through multiple channels: live chat, email support, and phone support. Premium users get priority access to our dedicated support team. Visit our support page for contact details.
What should I do if I can't install the software?
First, ensure your system meets the minimum requirements. Try running the installer as administrator and temporarily disable other security software. If issues persist, our support team can provide remote assistance.

Security & Privacy

How does Farway Security handle my personal data?
We take your privacy seriously. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely following industry best practices. We only collect necessary information for product functionality and never share your data with third parties without consent.
How often are security updates released?
Security updates are released automatically and regularly to ensure protection against the latest threats. Critical security patches are pushed immediately when new threats are detected.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any additional questions or concerns.
